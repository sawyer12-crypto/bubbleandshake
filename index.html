Bubble and Shake – Catering Terms & Conditions

Pricing

1) Pricing

  • 350ml Fruit Tea with Popping Boba – K60
  • 500ml Fruit Tea with Popping Boba – K80
  • 350ml Milk Tea with Tapioca – K70
  • 500ml Milk Tea with Tapioca – K90

2) Minimum Order

A minimum of 20 drinks per order is required.

Flavor Selection

  • Maximum of 4 flavors per 20 drinks.
  • Additional flavors may be subject to approval and availability.

3) Order Notice

All catering orders must be placed at least 48 hours in advance.

5) Event Setup Option

We offer on-site setup for events.

Please send an inquiry in advance for availability, pricing, and setup requirements.

6) Delivery & Collection

Delivery is available at an additional fee (based on location).

7) Payment Terms

  • Full payment is required to confirm all catering orders.
  • Orders are only secured once payment has been received.

8) Changes & Cancellations

  • Any changes must be requested at least 24 hours in advance to be guaranteed.
  • Changes requested within 24 hours are not guaranteed and will be subject to availability.
  • Late cancellations may not be accommodated. No refunds.

9) Product Quality

  • Drinks are best consumed fresh. We recommend serving immediately upon delivery or pickup.
  • Bubble and Shake is not responsible for quality once drinks have been held for extended periods after collection or delivery.
